How to Play

Premier League Darts combines football knowledge with darts scoring!

  • Objective: Be the first player to reach exactly 0 points (or closest to 0 without going below -20)
  • Starting Score: Both players begin with 501 points
  • Gameplay: Each turn, you'll get a prompt asking you to name a football player with specific criteria
  • Scoring: The player's Premier League appearances become your darts score for that turn
  • Valid Scores: Only players with 180 or fewer appearances and valid darts scores are accepted
  • Turn Time: You have 60 seconds to submit a valid player name
  • Winning: First player to reach 0 (or closest to 0, minimum -20) wins the game!

Example: If the prompt asks for "a Forward from England" and you choose "Harry Kane" with 180 appearances, you score 180 points and your opponent's turn begins.
